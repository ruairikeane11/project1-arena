About Us

Arena Combat Ireland is Ireland's newest kick-boxing promotiion. Our goal is to host the highest level of kick-boxing bouts throughout the country. We host both K1, and muay-thai style fights, and we bring you the best amateur fighters throguhout Ireland from each discipline.

Rule-sets

Muay-Thai

  • Elbows and knees below collar
  • All strikes allowed in clinch
  • 10oz gloves/No shin pads
  • 3 minute rounds

K1

  • No elbows
  • Only 1 strike allowed in clinch
  • 10oz gloves/shin pads
  • 2 minute rounds
